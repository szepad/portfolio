Rogue Melee

Rogue Melee is an action Roguelike where you fight your way through a randomly generated dungeon. As you descend deeper, every floor has a unique theme, cast of enemies, bosses and treasure to find. Additionally, you will also acquire stronger weapons and items that empower you on your journey.

Technical Details

Developed with C# using Raylib framework.

  • Over 35.000 lines of code in about 379 files
  • Clean and concise code base
  • 2D Real-time lightning with HLSL shaders
  • Efficient and precise collision detection
  • Custom Pathfinding
